Detailed Description

Bounds for a cubical Volume, the orientedSurfaces(...) method creates a vector of 6 surfaces:

BoundarySurfaceFace [index]:

  • negativeFaceXY [0] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, parallel to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [1] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, parallel to plane at positive
  • negativeFaceXY [2] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, attached to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [3] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, attached to plane at negative
  • negativeFaceXY [4] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, parallel to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [5] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface, parallel to plane at positive
